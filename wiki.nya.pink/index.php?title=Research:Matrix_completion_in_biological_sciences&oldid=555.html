Remember Jurassic Park? They recovered dinosaur DNA using frog DNA to fill in the gaps. Isn't that matrix completion? (See [1] and the video included). Maybe it can also be used to design organisms using genetic modification? For example, can make a matrix where on one side we would have properties of an organism, and on another organism itself (which corresponds to its DNA).
